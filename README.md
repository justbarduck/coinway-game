# Coinway

**Coinway** is a web-based, fast-paced arcade racing game developed by **Barduck Studio**. Built entirely using modern web tech including HTML5 Canvas and Tailwind CSS, the game challenges players to steer high-tech vehicles down a futuristic highway, weaving past traffic and obstacles while gathering coins and survival pick-ups.

---

## 🏁 Game Modes

* **Endless Race Mode:** Drive for as long as your resources permit. You can choose between three customizable difficulties—Easy, Normal, and Extreme—which scale resource drain, top speed, and your payout multipliers accordingly.


* **NPC Race Mode (Competitions):** Pit your skills directly against AI rival cars across four distinct brackets: Easy, Normal, Hard, and Expert. Compete against a strict 60-second timer and your opponent's progressive distance to claim massive flat-rate coin prizes. Entering an NPC competition consumes an attempt; attempts max out at 3 and refresh via an integrated 4-hour cooldown timer.



---

## 🚗 Unlockable Vehicles

Manage your roster in the Garage screen, where you can buy and swap between four unique car models using your earned currency:

* **Starter:** The default baseline vehicle available from the beginning at no cost.


* **Supercar:** Available for 15,000 coins, granting a 1.25x speed modifier and a 1.5x income generation multiplier.


* **Formula F1:** Available for 50,000 coins, delivering an aggressive 1.7x speed modifier and a 2.5x income generation multiplier.


* **Hovercar:** The ultimate model available for 120,000 coins, providing a massive 2.0x speed multiplier and a 3.0x income generation multiplier.



---

## 🛠️ Upgrades & Modules

Optimize your vehicle performance dynamically by buying level upgrades for five key components in the Garage:

* **Engine:** Incrementally improves the car's baseline top speed.


* **Battery:** Expands your maximum starting energy/fuel pool to extend your runs.


* **Hull Integrity:** Bolsters structural health metrics, allowing your vehicle to survive extra crash impacts.


* **Magnet:** Magnetizes your car, widening the collection radius to vacuum up distant coins automatically.


* **Coin Multiplier:** Permanently expands the economic return rate of all captured coins.



---

## 📜 Quest System

The game rewards long-term progression with a dedicated Quest log featuring milestone payouts:

* **Distance Milestones:** Earn up to 20,000 bonus coins by achieving long-term progressive travel distance goals from 25,000m to 100,000m.


* **Earning Milestones:** Secure up to 35,000 bonus coins by hitting cumulative income targets ranging up to 100,000 total lifetime coins earned.



---

## 🎮 Interface & Controls

* **Dual Camera Mode:** Toggle your perspective on the fly between a pseudo-3D highway horizon simulation or a classic retro top-down arcade view.


* **Nitro Propulsion:** Capturing coins charges up your specialized Nitro core. Reaching 100% capacity allows you to deploy a massive thruster burst that multiplies your speed by 1.8x and broadens your magnet radius by 2.5x for 4 seconds.


* **Steering:** Guide your car smoothly by moving your desktop mouse left/right across the display or dragging your thumb along touch-compatible screens.


* **Nitro Activation:** Trigger your speed boost manually by clicking the radial on-screen Nitro button, or by triggering a quick double-click anywhere inside the active canvas area.


* **On-Board Diagnostics (HUD):** Keep track of critical metrics via a real-time cluster showing active coins, distance traveled, Energy remaining, Hull Integrity, and your current Nitro level.


* **Procedural Sound FX:** Utilizes the Web Audio API to synthesize low-latency sound effects on the fly—generating distinct arcade sound profiles for item pick-ups, impacts, clicks, and thrusters.
